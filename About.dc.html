About Cadence

Built to listen.

Cadence began with a simple belief: your body is already telling you how it's doing — every night, in signals too quiet to notice. We built a companion that listens, and hands the meaning back to you each morning.

Our mission

Earlier insight, in plain language, for everyone.

Too much health technology answers questions you didn't ask with numbers you can't read. Cadence does the opposite. It watches the quiet rhythms of your sleep, heart and breath, notices when something gently shifts against your own baseline, and tells you one clear thing — with a next step you control. It spots patterns; it doesn't diagnose.

The night-to-dawn principle

One idea carries through everything we make.

Night is vigilance

While you rest, Cadence keeps a calm watch — deep indigo, quiet, never anxious.

Dawn is clarity

Each morning the watch becomes a warm, readable insight you can act on — and share.

The waveform is our heartbeat

A single living biosignal line runs through the product as a visible pulse — used sparingly, so it stays meaningful.
What we believe

Principles we don't bend on.

Calm over alarm.
Anomalies are a small shift to watch, never a red siren. We choose soft pairs and plain verbs.
Relative to you, always.
Every metric is read against your own baseline — not a population average or a verdict.
Your data is yours.
Encrypted, never sold. You decide what to share, with whom, and you can stop anytime.
We inform, we don't diagnose.
Cadence helps you walk into a clinician's visit knowing more — it never replaces one.
