Privacy

Calm, honest, and yours alone.

Your body's signals are some of the most personal data there is. We treat them that way. Here's how Cadence handles your information — in plain language.

The short version

Four promises we build around.

Encrypted end to end

Your signals are encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is tightly limited and audited.

Never sold

We don't sell or rent your data, and we don't run ads against it. Our business is the subscription, not you.

You control sharing

Share a report with a clinician or caregiver only when you choose — and revoke it whenever you like.

Non-diagnostic

Cadence surfaces patterns to help you and your clinician — it isn't a medical device and doesn't diagnose.
In more detail

The plain-language version.

What we collect

The biometric signals from devices you connect — heart rate, heart-rate variability, sleep stages, breathing rate — plus the account details you give us, like your email. We collect only what's needed to produce your insights.

How we use it

To read your signals against your own baseline, surface early shifts, and write the plain-language insight you see each morning. We use aggregated, de-identified data to improve how Cadence reads patterns — never to identify you to anyone.

Your rights

You can export your data, correct it, or delete your account and everything in it at any time. Reach us and we'll act promptly — questions about your data are always welcome at our contact page.

This summary is written for clarity and isn't a substitute for the full policy. Cadence spots patterns — it doesn't diagnose.
